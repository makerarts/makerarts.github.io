Our Provisions


Employability Programme



The next steps into further education or careers can be daunting. Aimed at those in upper secondary school our bespoke employability programme encouraged young people to research job opportunities, try out ‘simulated work’ roles in our purpose build work environments and well as out in our community. In addition to CV writing, interview skills all delivered in a fun, engaging and creative way whilst working towards gaining qualifications.

Arts Programme



At Maker Arts we believe creativity can help encourage young people to learn new skills but also have fun learning techniques and styles. We are able to facilitate many different types of art from painting and drawing, to clay and print, fabric and mosaics and young people to achieve an Arts Award certificate by Trinity.


Creative Science



Our Creative Science sessions provide an excellent way of engaging students with science and STEM whilst supporting their learning, embedding knowledge and achieving Crest certificates. We aim to spark imagination and support them to explore all of the possibilities through innovation and creativity.
