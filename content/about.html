About us

Maker Arts CIC is a community interest company based in the heart of Paignton, Torbay.

We offer small groups of students aged 9-16 bespoke courses in mindfulness, employability, art and science.

Our aims:

To develop confidence

Facilitate well-being

Encourage aspirations

We are particularly interested in supporting those that are anxious, phobic or otherwise disadvantaged.

Our end goal is to encourage these students back into the classroom.



Meet the team

Sue

Sue is Level 5 Certified in Education and Training with over 8 years experience in the education sector in a variety of settings. Sue has a wealth of experience working with young people, specifically in the areas of re-engagement and NEET, as well as mainstream education settings. Sue is passionate about helping young people to prepare for adult life with a caring, creative and inovative approach.

Abi

Abi is a qualified Science teacher with QTS status, over 20 years of teaching experience in a variety of settings and 5+ years experience of working one to one with children who have been diagnosed with ASD and ADHD. Abi also tutors Science to GCSE and Chemistry A level part time, both to students in school and those who are home educated.
