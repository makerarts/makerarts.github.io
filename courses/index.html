Courses



We offer three Arts Awards from Trinity College London. Arts Award inspires young people to grow their arts and leadership talents: it’s creative, valuable and accessible.

Qualifications

Discover: An introductory award aimed at young people under 25.

Explore: An entry Level 3 award aimed at young people aged 8 to 16.

Bronze: The Bronze Arts Award is a Level 1 qualification on the Regulated
Qualifications Framework (RQF) and is open to young people aged 11 to 25.



CREST is a nationally recognised scheme for student-led project work in the STEM subjects (science, technology, engineering and maths). We offer two CREST Awards.

Qualifications

Bronze: An introduction to STEM project work for 11+ year olds working individually or in teams.

Silver: Challenge your 14+ year-old students to run their own STEM projects in teams or individually.



Employability and Entrepreneurship

A fun and creative bespoke program designed by Maker Arts for young people in Key Stage 4 or those at risk of/already not in Education, Employment or Training (NEET). This course provides the foundations for team working, communication skills, organisation and problem solving skills that future employees need to propel them towards their dream career.
